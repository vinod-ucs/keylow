Feature 01 — Inventory + SKU

Unique SKUs, automatic and traceable.

Every inventory item receives a unique 8-character SKU the moment it enters Software. Warehouse operations become searchable, filterable and completely traceable — without a single line of a spreadsheet.

  • Automatic 8-character SKU generation
  • Advanced inventory filters by event, tag & location
  • Assigned / unassigned status tracking
  • Search by SKU, title or ASIN
  • One-click CSV export
