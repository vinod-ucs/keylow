FEATURE 02

Barcode + Webcam Scanning

Capture product information instantly using your browser's webcam. Scan any barcode and LotVault automatically retrieves product details, images and pricing from Amazon, eliminating manual inventory entry.

01 Open Add Item and select Barcode Lookup.
02 Launch the built-in webcam scanner.
03 Scan product barcode in seconds.
04 Amazon API fills product title, images and MSRP automatically.
No Hardware Required

Works directly inside the browser using WebRTC. No external barcode scanner is needed.

QR Code Generation Per Inventory Item

Every inventory item automatically receives a downloadable QR code containing its SKU, title, sourced-by information and MSRP. Staff can scan the code anywhere in the warehouse to instantly retrieve the complete inventory record without manual searching.
